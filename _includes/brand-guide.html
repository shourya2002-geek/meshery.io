Brand Guide

To begin with, a brand guide has been composed to inform on appropriate usage of all of Layer5’s assets and toolkit, Meshery included. It contains instructions on when, where, and how to make use of Meshery assets including logos and watermarks, even discussing more extensive use cases and providing directives where necessary. To access all of this information, simply access the Layer5 Brand Guide and for component specific directives, visit Layer5’s design system, Sistent Design System at design.layer5.io
